Due to the increasing number of Spam mails we had to re-configure or mail server to take only mails from officially registered mail exchanger domains. This means the mail server contacting our host is able to identify itself and can be traced down to a responsible individual. All mail hosts operated by a responsible Internet Service Provider should be able to identify itself. Unfortunately there are still a number of misconfigured mail hosts on the Internet. If you get a mail back saying the your mail could not be delivered then you might want to contact your ISP on this issue.
